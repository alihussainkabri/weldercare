Welldercare aims to be the Gold Standard in Person Centered Curated Care by holistically addressing the needs of an ageing demographic - from a 'pre-need' stage in life by addressing psychosocial needs to lifestyle support and intensive attendant care through a convergence of cognitive behavioural design-based care & superior technology.
We are pioneering India's first 'Lifestyle As A Service' (LAAS) model in Later Life Living, offering a revolutionary alternative to the traditional real-estate driven retirement homes. Where our clients get to choose from our network of resorts across India that combine world-class hospitality, healthcare, and wellness for a service fee. This model offers an all-inclusive later life living solution which is flexible and suited for the 60 plus without a heavy investment.
In addition to keeping you safe and protected, we bring our cutting edge technology suite – Care Shield – to your home at an easily affordable monthly subscription to track all your health parameters and detect any anomalies non-invasively, giving you access to the best doctors and healthcare infrastructure in your city. Care Shield comes with life saving features like being ever alert for falls and ensuring a lightning speed response in the unlikely event of a fall in your home, especially in your bathroom - 86% of falls in a bathroom that go undetected within an hour often result in fatalities.
